Project Description:

This project features the 2017 water crisis in Cape Town city in South Africa. The analysis utilizes Python's pandas and folium libraries. The map shows the 2017 average water Consumption per suburb in the month of October. Users can select points showing pop-up of the suburb name and water Consumption per kilolitre.
